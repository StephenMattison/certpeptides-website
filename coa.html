Quality Assurance & Certificates of Analysis

Every peptide we sell is independently tested — transparency and purity are non-negotiable

Our Commitment to Quality

At AssayPeptides, quality is the foundation of everything we do. Every batch of research peptides undergoes rigorous third-party laboratory testing before it reaches your lab. We publish full certificates of analysis (COA) for complete transparency — because researchers deserve to know exactly what they're working with.

Testing Methodology

Each peptide is analyzed using industry-standard analytical methods:

  • High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC): Confirms peptide purity to ≥98–99% for every batch.
  • Mass Spectrometry (MS): Verifies molecular weight and structural identity of each compound.
  • Endotoxin Testing (LAL): Ensures products meet acceptable endotoxin levels for research applications.
  • Sterility Testing: Bacteriostatic water and reconstitution supplies undergo sterility verification.

Third-Party Independence

All testing is performed by accredited, independent analytical laboratories — never in-house. This ensures unbiased, verifiable results that you can trust for your research. Our testing partners hold ISO 17025 accreditation and follow GLP (Good Laboratory Practice) standards.

What Each COA Includes

  • Product name and batch/lot number
  • Purity percentage (HPLC)
  • Molecular weight confirmation (MS)
  • Appearance and physical description
  • Date of analysis and expiration
  • Laboratory name and analyst signature
