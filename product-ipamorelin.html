Product Description

Ipamorelin is a synthetic pentapeptide with the sequence Aib-His-D-2Nal-D-Phe-Lys-NH2. Developed by Novo Nordisk, it is classified as a growth hormone secretagogue (GHS) and ghrelin receptor agonist. Unlike other GHS compounds such as GHRP-6 or GHRP-2, Ipamorelin is highly selective for growth hormone release and does not significantly stimulate the secretion of cortisol, prolactin, or adrenocorticotropic hormone (ACTH) at physiologically relevant concentrations. This selectivity makes it a valuable research tool for studying the GH/IGF-1 axis in isolation, without confounding effects from other pituitary hormones.
