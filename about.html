About CertPeptides

Columbia, Missouri's trusted source for premium research peptides

Our Mission

CertPeptides was founded with a clear mission: to provide the scientific research community with the highest-quality peptides available, backed by rigorous third-party testing and full certificates of analysis. We believe that groundbreaking research starts with reliable, pure materials — and that's exactly what we deliver.

Based in Columbia, Missouri

Headquartered in the heart of Columbia, MO — home to the University of Missouri and a thriving research corridor — CertPeptides is strategically positioned to serve researchers across the United States. Our central location enables efficient, fast shipping to virtually any domestic destination.

Being rooted in a university town means we understand the demands of academic and independent research. We built our business to meet the exacting standards that scientists depend on every day.

Our Quality Standards

Quality isn't an aspiration at CertPeptides — it's a requirement. Every peptide in our catalog undergoes:

  • 99%+ Purity Verification: Independent HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) analysis confirms peptide purity on every batch.
  • Mass Spectrometry Confirmation: Molecular weight and structural identity are verified using state-of-the-art mass spectrometry instrumentation.
  • Third-Party Independence: All analytical testing is performed by accredited, external laboratories — never in-house — to ensure objectivity and accuracy.
  • Full Traceability: Every product includes a batch number and lot-specific certificate of analysis for complete supply chain transparency.

Who We Serve

Our customers include university research laboratories, independent research institutions, biotechnology companies, and qualified individual researchers. We supply peptides exclusively for legitimate scientific and laboratory research purposes. Our products are not intended for human or animal consumption.

Our Product Range

CertPeptides offers a carefully curated catalog of research peptides spanning multiple categories:

  • GLP-1 Agonists: Tirzepatide, Semaglutide, Retatrutide — for metabolic and incretin signaling research.
  • Healing & Recovery: BPC-157, TB-500 — for tissue repair and wound healing studies.
  • Growth Hormone Secretagogues: Sermorelin, CJC-1295, Ipamorelin — for GH axis and pulsatile secretion research.
  • Anti-Aging Peptides: GHK-Cu, Epitalon — for telomere, longevity, and cellular aging research.
  • Melanocortin Peptides: PT-141 — for neuroendocrine and melanocortin receptor pathway studies.
