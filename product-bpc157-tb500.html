Product Description

The BPC-157 + TB-500 Blend combines two of the most extensively researched regenerative peptides into a single lyophilized vial. BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound-157) is a 15-amino acid pentadecapeptide derived from gastric juice proteins, studied for its role in gastrointestinal protection and wound healing.
